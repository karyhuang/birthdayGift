Let's play a little game, shall we?


  1. Write down an 8 digit number composed of your birth year & your computer pin
  2. Add "11" to the 8 digit number to get a new 8 digit number
  3. Using odd numbers as rows and even numbers as columns, find the 4 letter word represented by your 8 digit number (for example, 1234 would be row 1 col 2 and row 3 col 4)
  4. Got your 4 letter word? Now go back to the form and enter in the textbox.
